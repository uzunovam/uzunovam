I am a PPE PhD candidate/Junior lecturer at the John Stuart Mill College, Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam. My research (supervised by Prof. Dr. René van den Brink and Prof. Dr. Martin van Hees) lies at the intersection of philosophy and economics and concerns—conceptual, methodological, and normative—issues of social power. For these and other issues I think and write about (often, with co-authors), I use game theory, social networks, and social choice theory. The history of economic and philosophical thought are two areas of, if not specialisazation, then fascination—there, I try to keep up and learn from others as best one can.

I am also one of the editors of the Erasmus Journal for Philosophy and Economics—completely open-access and with an expressed commitment to fostering the work of junior scholars.
